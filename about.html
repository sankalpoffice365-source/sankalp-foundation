About Sankalp Inclusive Foundation

Sankalp Inclusive Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to creating meaningful impact through compassionate programs. Founded on the principles of compassion, inclusion, and excellence, we work to create opportunities for growth, learning, and independence.

Currently, our foundation operates Sankalp Learning Center, our flagship educational program for neurodiverse children. As we grow, we envision expanding our reach to serve other communities in need, including elderly care, cancer support, and other areas where our mission of compassion and support can make a meaningful difference. We are governed by a dedicated board of directors and supported by a team of passionate educators, therapists, and advocates.
