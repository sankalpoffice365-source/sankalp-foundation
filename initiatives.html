Our Programs

Sankalp Inclusive Foundation was created to eventually support a variety of programs serving children and families. However, at this stage, the Sankalp Learning Center is our primary and foundational initiative. Until we establish strong operations, staffing, and community presence, the foundation and the learning center will function as a single integrated entity.

Sankalp Learning Center

Our primary program providing individualized, inclusive education and support for children of all abilities. We offer:

  • Individualized instruction
  • Structured learning + movement breaks
  • Hands-on math, science, and engineering
  • Communication-friendly classrooms
  • Low student-to-teacher ratio
  • Emotional regulation and SEL support
  • Life skills and independence building
  • Family guidance and collaboration
  • TEKS-aligned curriculum adapted for diverse learners
