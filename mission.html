Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive, and nurturing learning environment where every child—regardless of ability—can thrive academically, emotionally, and socially.

We are committed to:

  • Providing tailored instruction and support
  • Encouraging communication and self-expression
  • Creating emotionally safe environments
  • Supporting families with guidance and resources
  • Empowering each child to learn and grow with dignity

Sankalp Learning Center is powered by Sankalp Inclusive Foundation, and together they represent a single, unified commitment to children and families.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive educational community where children with diverse needs can learn in ways that honor their individuality. We envision a future where every child has access to meaningful education, support, and opportunities—where families feel understood, supported, and empowered.

As Sankalp Inclusive Foundation grows, we may expand into additional community programs, but today our full focus is on building the Sankalp Learning Center as a strong, sustainable educational model for children with diverse learning needs.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every individual deserves care, respect, and understanding.
  • Inclusion: We create spaces where everyone belongs and can contribute.
  • Excellence: We maintain high standards in education and support services.
  • Collaboration: We work together with families, educators, and communities.
  • Growth: We believe in continuous learning and development for all.
