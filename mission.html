Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Sankalp Foundation USA is dedicated to empowering individuals and families through compassionate programs that address diverse needs. Currently, our focus is on Sankalp Learning Center, which provides comprehensive educational programs and therapeutic services for neurodiverse children and their families. As we grow, we envision expanding our reach to serve other communities in need, including elderly care, cancer support, and other areas where compassion and support can make a meaningful difference.

Our Vision

To build a world where every individual has access to the support, education, and care they need to thrive. We envision a future where compassion and understanding guide all interactions, and where communities come together to ensure no one is left behind—whether through educational programs, healthcare support, elder care, or other essential services.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every individual deserves care, respect, and understanding.
  • Inclusion: We create spaces where everyone belongs and can contribute.
  • Excellence: We maintain high standards in education and support services.
  • Collaboration: We work together with families, educators, and communities.
  • Growth: We believe in continuous learning and development for all.
