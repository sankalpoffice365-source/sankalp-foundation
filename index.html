Empowering lives through compassion and action.

Sankalp Learning Center is the primary program and the heart of the Sankalp Inclusive Foundation, the nonprofit organization that operates it. At this stage, both names represent the same mission and work: providing individualized, inclusive education and support for children of all abilities. As we grow, the foundation may expand into additional programs, but today our full commitment is building and strengthening the learning center.
